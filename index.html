The Global Game Jam is a 48-hour game jam hosted in 600+ locations in 90+ countries worldwide. On January 20th, 2017, thousands of game developers, programmers, artists and musicians come together to create thousands of unique games - all over the course of one weekend! This year, GGJ is happening on uVic campus on the 6th floor of the engineering building!

Free Food. Endless Games. Long nights, new ideas, and a chance to collaborate on something unique.

That's the Global Game Jam!
