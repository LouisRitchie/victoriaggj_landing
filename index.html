the uvic gamedev club presents

The University of Victoria Game Development Club presents the 2017 Global Game Jam! The Global Game Jam is a 48-hour game jam hosted in over 600 locations in over 90 countries worldwide. On January 20th, 2017, thousands of game developers, programmers, artists and musicians come together to create thousands of unique games - all over the course of one weekend! This year, GGJ is happening right in the heart of Downtown Victoria, in the Rocket Lounge inside Fort Tectoria on 777 Fort St. Free Food. Endless Games. Your own GGJ t-shirt. Long nights, new ideas, and collaborating on something unique. This is the Global Game Jam. Register now and be a part of the action!
