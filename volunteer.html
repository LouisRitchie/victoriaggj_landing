Volunteering for the Global Game Jam

  • Louis Ritchie is the event organizer. Any questions you have should be directed to him either in the UVic GameDev Club slack channel, by email at louiscritchie@gmail.com, or to the UVGD club email at uvgd@uvic.ca.
  • We need volunteers to help with registration, directing jammers (participants), setting up and taking down AV equipment, and helping jammers with any questions they have regarding the structure of the game jam. There are important deadlines for registering teams and submitting their games, so part of your job will be making sure our jammers know when these deadlines are.
  • In exchange for your volunteering, you'll be fed and given the same t-shirt as participants recieve except in the case that we run out of food or t-shirts. Jammers are paying $25 to participate and so they get top priority for being fed and getting a shirt. That said, unless there is unexpectedly large turnout, you should be able to get a shirt and a meal after your volunteering shift.
  • Volunteers may sign up for one or more of the following shifts:
    1. SHIFT A: Jan 20, 1pm - 9pm
    2. SHIFT B: Jan 21, 8am - 4pm
    3. SHIFT C: Jan 21, 4pm - 12pm
    4. SHIFT D: Jan 22, 8am - 4pm
    5. SHIFT E: Jan 22, 4pm - 7pm
